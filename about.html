About Me

Hi, I'm Aiden Corbin. I'm currently studying a double degree in Mechatronics and Biomedical Engineering at the Queensland University of Technology (QUT).

I am fascinated by the intersection of hardware and software. Whether it's developing control systems for robotics, reading biometric sensors, or building web-dashboards to monitor embedded devices, I love creating systems that interact with the physical world.

This website serves as my digital garden. It's a place where I document my university projects, personal builds, and host a live rendering of my Markdown notes for easy reference.
